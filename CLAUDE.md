# Turby — Project Guide

## Design Context

Turby is a warm, editorial experience — a house journal for housemates and visitors to browse at leisure. Apply these principles only when working on **UI, layout, or visual design**. Don't apply them to logic, data, or 3D scene work.

**Brand:** Warm · Editorial · Grounded

**Principles (for UI work only):**
- Ivy Presto Headline is the primary display face — use it large and light, give it room
- Whitespace sets reading pace — be generous with padding and line-height
- Hierarchy through scale and spacing, not weight
- Controls should be subtle and unobtrusive
- Soft tones and gentle transitions over sharp or clinical aesthetics
