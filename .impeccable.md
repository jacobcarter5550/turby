# Turby — Impeccable Design Context

## Design Context

### Users
Housemates and visitors exploring info about their home, the people who live there, and what's happening. Usage is casual and leisurely — browsing between articles and images with a spirit of discovery rather than urgency.

### Brand Personality
**Warm. Editorial. Grounded.**
Turby feels like a beautifully produced house journal — personal, considered, and unhurried. It invites you to slow down and look.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual tone:** Airy and editorial — generous whitespace, premium serif typography (Ivy Presto), soft image treatment
- **Reference feel:** A high-quality print magazine or a thoughtfully designed personal site
- **Anti-references:** Avoid dashboard-y, corporate, or overly minimal/cold aesthetics
- **Theme:** Light interiors for dialogs and reading surfaces; the 3D scene provides the drama

### Design Principles
1. **Let the type lead.** Ivy Presto Headline is the soul of the brand — use it large and light for titles, and give it room to breathe
2. **Whitespace is content.** Padding and line-height are not decorative; they set the reading pace
3. **Warmth over perfection.** Soft tones, rounded edges, and gentle transitions over sharp or clinical precision
4. **Hierarchy through scale, not weight.** Differentiate elements by size and spacing, keeping weights light
5. **Invisible chrome.** Navigation and controls should feel subtle — present when needed, not competing with content
